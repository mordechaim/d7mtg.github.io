Curesse Medi Spa

  SERVICES

In a world where challenges abound and mental health is precarious, self-care is put on a pedestal and is recommended by all. A medispa is a perfect place where anyone can escape to relax, destress and feel taken care of. Today more than ever, the need for enhancing self-care and self-esteem is high up on the priority list.

This brand showcases a harmonious blend of sophistication and warmth, both crucial for the brand's success. Curesse medispa is a spa that specializes in mental and physical wellness. It provides peace of mind and space for clients to focus on themselves and find the time and space to take care of themselves and their mental health.

Curesse is the type of place you can come to after a long day when you need to destress and detox.

We wanted to achieve an inviting, soft, yet empowering look for the type. We used a lettering style with a graceful feeling but still a strong presence.

For the icon, we started with a circular shape to represent the idea of wholeness, completeness, and organization. We drew inspiration from the simple things, the physical space surrounding us: the silhouette of a sofa - a place where we can feel comfortable and at ease; a leaf - a symbol of rejuvenation and growth.

The slogan "reclaim your glow" is a nod to the idea that life can be overwhelming sometimes but that you can always find joy, excitement, and happiness.
