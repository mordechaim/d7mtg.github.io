BHY Property Management

  SERVICES

BHY Property Management is a high-end property management company based in New Jersey.

They've been in the business for years, and have a strong reputation for providing quality services.

BHY Property Management approached us to help them create a new branding identity and logo, as their old branding was starting to look a little dated.

We worked together to create a new logo that is based around a simple circle, and we created custom lettering that is based in geometric shapes.

The new visuals reflect the luxurious and high-end nature of the company, and the navy and rose gold colors give it a rich and sophisticated feel.
